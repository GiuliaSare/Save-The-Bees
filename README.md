## Study Objectives
Analyzing the health trends of honey bee populations in the US, focusing on the impacts of climate change, parasites, and diseases.  
**Research Question**: *What is the health status trend of honey bee populations in the US, and what threats endanger them?*

## Dataset
- **Source**: USDA dataset (2015-2022) published on Kaggle.
- **Rows**: 1453 | **Columns**: 17 (no missing values)
- **Key Variables**:
  - `num_colonies`: Total colonies
  - `lost_colonies`: Lost colonies and percentage (`percent_lost`)
  - Contributing factors: `varroa_mites`, `diseases`, `pesticides`, etc.

## Data Preprocessing
Used Tableau's calculated fields to create new columns:
- **Added+Renovated Colonies**
- **Quarter_Date**: Date format for quarters
- **Year_stringa**: Year as string

Interactive elements include:
- **Map and Bubble charts** for colony data.

- **Initial Graphs**: Discarded overlapping area graphs.
- **Color Adjustments**: Changed to brown shades for clarity.
- **Refined Visuals**: Created flower-shaped graphs to show variance between lost and added colonies.
- **Trend Analysis**: Used simple line charts to illustrate decreasing colony losses over time.
